Caipirinha - the traditional brazilian cocktail!

What is Caipirinha?

Caipirinha is a traditional Brazilian cocktail, and one of the official drinks from IBA. It is served everywhere in the world, and one of the most popular drink in many coutries!

The drink is made with cachaça, which is a spirit made of sugar cane. It is similar to rum, but made with a different process.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot of cachaça (50ml)
  • 1 lime
  • 2 scoops of sugar
  • ice, ice baby

Steps

  1. Cut the limes in small pieces, discarding the center white part.
  2. In a cocktail shaker, add the sugar and smash everything together.
  3. Add the ice and the cachaça.
  4. Shake it like a polaroid picture!!
